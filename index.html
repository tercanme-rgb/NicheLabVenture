Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2025  ·  NicheLab Research Ltd.

NicheLab is committed to handling your personal data with care and discretion. This policy describes what data we collect, how it is used, and the rights available to you under applicable law, including the GDPR.

1. Data We Collect

  • Account data: full name, email address, hashed password, account creation timestamp, tier status
  • Research activity: brief titles, categories selected, publication timestamps, usage within plan limits
  • Contact submissions: name, email, subject classification, and message content submitted via our contact form
  • Technical data: IP address, browser agent, operating system, session identifiers
  • Payment data: billing processed exclusively by Stripe; NicheLab retains only transaction metadata

2. Purpose of Processing

  • To authenticate accounts and enforce tier-based research access controls
  • To deliver, maintain, and improve NicheLab's research platform and methodology
  • To communicate service updates, billing changes, and new research capabilities
  • To respond to contact form submissions and support enquiries
  • To detect abuse, enforce our acceptable use policy, and ensure platform integrity
  • To comply with applicable legal obligations and regulatory requirements

3. Data Infrastructure & Security

All user data is stored on Supabase, a PostgreSQL-backed cloud infrastructure hosted within the European Union. Data is encrypted at rest using AES-256 and in transit via TLS 1.3. Row Level Security policies are enforced at the database layer to prevent cross-account data access.

4. Third-Party Data Sharing

  • Stripe: receives billing and payment data to process subscriptions
  • Research infrastructure providers: receive only research query content, never personally identifiable information
  • Legal authorities: data may be disclosed when required by Portuguese law, EU regulation, or a binding court order

NicheLab does not sell, rent, or trade your personal data under any circumstances.

5. Your Data Rights (GDPR)

  • Access: obtain a copy of all personal data we hold about you
  • Rectification: correct inaccurate or incomplete personal data
  • Erasure: request deletion of your account and all associated personal data
  • Restriction: limit certain categories of data processing
  • Portability: receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Objection: opt out of direct marketing at any time
  • Supervisory complaint: lodge a complaint with the CNPD (Portugal's data protection authority)

6. Cookie Policy

NicheLab uses only technically essential cookies for authentication session management. We do not use advertising cookies, cross-site tracking technologies, or third-party analytics.

7. Retention Periods

  • Account data: retained for the duration of the active subscription; deleted within 30 days of an erasure request
  • Research briefs: retained for 12 months from generation; export recommended prior to expiry
  • Contact form submissions: retained for 24 months
  • Payment and billing records: retained for 7 years per EU financial record-keeping obligations

8. Minors

NicheLab does not knowingly collect data from or provide services to individuals under the age of 18.

9. Policy Updates

This policy may be updated to reflect changes in our practices or applicable law. Substantive changes will be communicated to registered subscribers by email.

10. Data Controller & Contact

NicheLab Research Ltd. is the data controller for all personal data processed through this platform. For privacy requests, use our contact form or write to privacy@nichelab.io.
